Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety is generally characterized by feelings of tension, worrisome thoughts, and physical bodily sensations such as sweating or increased heart rate. People with this disorder often deal with recurring intrusive thoughts.

Psychotic Disorders

Psychotic disorders are mental illnesses that cause abnormal thoughts and perceptions that cause someone to lose touch with reality. Symptoms that are common with this disorder include delusions and hallucinations. Psychotic disorders can cause great distress and functional challenges in everyday life.
